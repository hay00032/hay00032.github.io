I am a 27 year old artist/designer from Niagara Falls, ON, but currently reside in Ottawa.

I was initially trained in the field of Fine Arts and once completing by degree from Brock University, I decided to dive into the design world and attempt to move my work from canvas to computer.

As an artist my personal work focuses on creations made by the hand and later developed by the machine. I enjoy utilizing illustration to my advantage because it allows me to embrace the irregular pencil lines, brush strokes, or anything that emulates the human presence. I have always been intrigued by the concept of what determines value, and how valuable a product is when it is produced by the hand or by machine.

I am intrigued by design as a whole, how concepts are devloped and how they are delivered, the boundaries that are pushed, and the constant evolution of what is trending. I became a designer to continue to learn and create things in new ways.
